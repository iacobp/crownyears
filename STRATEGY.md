# Crown Years — Ecosystem Strategy

## Architecture

**Mother site:** crownyears.com (or crownyears.vercel.app for now)
**Sub-sites:** Each vertical gets its own tool/resource site, linked from the mother.
**All on one Vercel project?** YES — use Next.js route groups or subdirectories. Everything under one repo, one deploy. Sub-domains can be added later when you buy the domain.

```
crownyears.com/                    → Mother brand, hub, newsletter
crownyears.com/resources           → All tools directory
crownyears.com/divorce             → Gray divorce tools (migrate from separate project)
crownyears.com/wardrobe            → Capsule wardrobe builder

crownyears.com/fitness             → Midlife fitness blueprint

```

## Monetization Streams

### 1. Affiliate Revenue (immediate)
**The big play.** She researches for 68 days before high-ticket purchases. Be the trusted recommendation source.

- **Wellness:** AG1, Oura Ring, supplements, fitness apps
- **Fashion:** Net-a-Porter, Nordstrom, The RealReal, Eileen Fisher
- **Beauty:** Trinny London, Augustinus Bader, Tata Harper, Drunk Elephant
- **Finance:** Financial advisor referrals, Betterment, Fidelity
- **Travel:** Booking.com, luxury hotels, retreat centers
- **Legal:** Divorce attorney directories, legal services
- **Telehealth:** Midi Health, Evernow, Alloy

Embed affiliate links naturally into every free tool and guide. The capsule wardrobe builder recommends specific products. The travel guide links to booking. The advocacy kit links to telehealth.

### 2. Digital Products (month 2+)
Sell via Gumroad or Lemonsqueezy (no inventory, instant delivery). Start with 2-3 products, expand based on what converts.

### 3. Newsletter Sponsorships (month 3+)
Once at 5K+ subscribers, brands will pay $500-$2,000 per send to reach this demographic. Email has the highest ROI ($36-40 per $1 spent) and this demo has the highest open rates (25.7%).

### 4. Premium Community (month 6+)
"Crown Circle" — $29/month. Monthly workshops, exclusive content, local meetup coordination, peer groups.

### 5. Brand Partnerships (year 1+)
Brands are desperate to reach this demo authentically. Sponsored content, product collaborations, consulting on how to market to midlife women.

## Competitive Moat

Why nobody else can easily replicate this:

1. **Integrated approach** — everyone else is siloed (menopause apps, divorce tools, fashion blogs). You connect everything.
2. **Free tools as trust builders** — competitors charge for worse tools. Free tools build audience that converts on higher-ticket items.
3. **Voice and permission** — the "sovereignty" framing is unique. Nobody talks to this woman like an equal with power.
4. **SEO first-mover** — the keywords are wide open. First quality content wins.
5. **Data-driven from day one** — Signal protocol means you're building what people actually complain about, not what you think they want.
